Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Information We Collect

This application accesses TikTok account data solely for the purpose of publishing video content. We do not collect or store personal data from TikTok users.

Account credentials and tokens are used only to authenticate with TikTok's API and publish content on behalf of the account owner.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored securely on the server and are not shared with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

This service integrates with TikTok's API. Your use of TikTok is subject to TikTok's own Privacy Policy.

5. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact the service operator.
